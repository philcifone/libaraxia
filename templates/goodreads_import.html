Import from Goodreads

Import your library from a Goodreads CSV export

Please Note

The Goodreads import is not perfect and will require some manual work:

  • Book covers will not be imported - You'll need to fetch them separately using the "Fetch Cover" feature
  • Missing book details - Some books may have incomplete information (publisher, page count, etc.)
  • Review formatting - Long reviews may need reformatting
  • Custom shelves - Only standard Goodreads shelves are recognized

Where Your Goodreads Data Goes

Wishlist

Books on your "to-read" or "want-to-read" shelves

Collections (Read)

Books on your "read" shelf

Includes: ratings, reviews, and reading completion dates

Collections (Currently Reading)

Books on your "currently-reading" shelf

Not Imported

Books on custom shelves or other non-standard shelves will be skipped

How to Export from Goodreads

  1. Go to Goodreads Import/Export
  2. Click "Export Library" at the top of the page
  3. Wait for the export to complete (you'll receive an email)
  4. Download the CSV file from the link in the email
  5. Upload it below
